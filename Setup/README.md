## Purchasing principles

- Confirm the exact board revision, processor, connector, voltage, and included parts.
- Compare local warranty and shipping costs against international storefront pricing.
- Treat storefront price and stock as live data; verify both immediately before buying.
- Prefer manufacturer documentation and schematics over seller descriptions.
- Buy USB data cables, suitable power supplies, headers, and current-limiting resistors
  with the board when they are not included.
- Use ESD controls and verify voltage with a multimeter before connecting a target.

Supplier entries are informational, not endorsements. Catalog observations include a
review date so they can be rechecked when products or availability change.

## Southeast Asia sourcing strategy

Shopee is often the practical first stop in Malaysia and Southeast Asia for
budget-friendly consumables, hand tools, cables, breakout boards, generic adapters,
and entry-level test equipment. Use specialist distributors where authenticity,
traceability, warranty, or product availability matters.

This repository treats the supplied specialist storefronts as exceptions rather than
the default source for every item:

- Argon40 and Cytron for official or less-common Raspberry Pi products
- Mouser Malaysia for the TIGARD V1 hardware-interface board
- Shopee for lower-cost generic equipment when the listing passes the checks below

Before buying from a marketplace, compare delivered cost and check seller history,
recent reviews with customer photos, exact model/revision, package contents, voltage
ratings, return policy, and local warranty. Treat phrases such as "original", "logic
analyser", "ST-LINK", or "FTDI" as unverified until supported by clear photos and
device markings.
